Three Golden Rules

1. Raise your hands when you speak 
When you have a question raising hands to 
speak helps to show you have a question.
2. No bad words 
Be friendly to others, no bad words or insults.
3. No nicknames unless permitted by others 
Be respectful, do not give people hurtful or meaningful
names when they don't like it.
4. Don't throw trash on the ground 
The classroom is public. We should all keep it clean.
5. No eating or drinking on the class 
Respect the teacher, and DO NOT drink or eat because you 
might spill them.
6. Keep quiet when watching videos or taking classes 
Keeping silent helps you to concentrate more.

Golden rule: RESPECT OTHERS
